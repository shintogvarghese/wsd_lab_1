Why you need to train your body?

The World Health Organization (WHO) had published an article in their official website, in the year 2016 which stated that the average life expectancy of a human being was 72.0 years and this figure will continue to fall over the coming years because of our busy and sedentary lifestyle, unhealthy food habits etc. The average human being would also face a lot of physical ailments in their old age such as weak joints, muscles and bones. Medical experts advise people to perform moderate amounts of weight training, stretchting along with proper nutrition on a regular basis to maintain a healthy lifestyle.
