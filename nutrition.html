Metabolism-Boosting Smoothies

1. Lime & Honey

  1. Take 1 lime and 600ml of water.
  2. Put them into a blender and blend well.
  3. Add some organic honey into a glass.
  4. Strain the mixture into the glass, stir and enjoy

2. Lime & Carrot

  • Take 1 lime, 1 carrot and 600ml of water.
  • Put them into a blender and blend well.
  • Add some organic honey into a glass.
  • Strain the mixture into the glass, stir and enjoy
